### Supported Classes

- `flexy-switch`

- `thumb-shadow`
- `thumb-shape`
- `thumb-width`

- `selected-icon-color`
- `disabled-unselected-track-color`
- `disabled-unselected-thumb-color`
- `disabled-unselected-icon-color`
- `disabled-selected-track-color`
- `disabled-selected-thumb-color`
- `disabled-selected-icon-color`
